How to Play

  • Move Left & Right: Left & Right Arrow Key
  • Jump:Spacebar
  • Throw Bottle: D Key (if you have bottles)
  • Collect Coins & Bottles: Walk over them
  • Defeat Enemies: Jump on them or throw bottles
  • Goal: Defeat the Endboss to win!

Tip: Avoid enemy attacks and save bottles to defeat the Endboss!
